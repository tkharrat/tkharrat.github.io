Tarak Kharrat

Lead Data Scientist at Real Analytics

I am a trained mathematician trying to educate computers to understand data. I started interrogating data seriously in 2010 when I was trying to build automated trading strategy to beat the US equity market. In 2012, I read my first paper about football analytics and since then I am dedicating most of working hours to this field. I am mostly interested in problems where accurate predictions are required (the outcome of a football game, the final position of the team in a league, …) and if possible where a strong benchmark is available. After few years in Academia (mainly as a research fellow) and various working experiences (big corporations as well as early stage start-up), I decided recently to work full time on an ambitious project I started with another academic colleague. Our mission is to make cutting edge research in the field available to decision-makers in football for better recruitment.
